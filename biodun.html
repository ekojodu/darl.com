ABOUT ME

Emmanuel Abiodun Abioye, Ph.D.

I am a scientist and engineer specializing in multidisciplinary research projects aimed at enhancing efficiency and reducing production costs, thereby promoting resilient and sustainable agriculture. My work is data-driven and focuses on the intersection of machine learning, precision agriculture, agricultural robotics, model predictive control, and system identification to address agricultural challenges. I instruct and supervise undergraduate and graduate students majoring in these fields, as well as interns from diverse backgrounds.
